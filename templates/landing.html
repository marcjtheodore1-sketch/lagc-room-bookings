LAGC - Fridays @ Farringdon

London Autism Group Charity Weekly Initiative

This form is to register yourself for the London Autism Group Charity's 'LAGC Fridays @ Farringdon' weekly 11am-4pm initiative.

We're pleased to share that we've secured space at Pan MacMillan's Head Office as part of a new ongoing Fridays initiative.

📍 Venue

Pan Macmillan HQ

View on Google Maps →

The building is approximately a 5-minute walk from Farringdon Station (Cowcross Street exit).

♿

The building is wheelchair accessible. The environment is calm, quiet and comfortable.

👥

There will be at least two LAGC volunteers present throughout the day to support and coordinate the space.

📝

This is a corporate building and security requires us to provide a list of attendees in advance. When you arrive, you will need to sign in at reception.

⚠️ Important: If you are not registered in advance, you may not be permitted entry.

This initiative is for autistic adults aged 18 and over only.

📝 Registration Notice: Please register yourself only. Do not register on behalf of others unless there are capacity or access issues that make independent registration difficult.

If you do register on behalf of someone else, please ensure they receive a copy of our community guidelines linked at the bottom of this page.

🏢 Our Space at Pan Macmillan

During our Fridays @ Farringdon initiative, LAGC has access to a variety of spaces at Pan Macmillan HQ. Please note: the specific rooms available vary from week to week. Each Friday, different rooms from the list below may be open for use. When you register, you'll see which rooms are available for that particular Friday.

🌊 Room 4.2 "Indigo" - FOR SHARED USE

Quiet Working & Body-Doubling Space

A shared calm room for focused work, planning, admin, studying or simply working alongside others quietly.

View Photo →

🌿 Room 4.4 "Rose" - FOR INDIVIDUAL USE

Private Focus Room

A quiet single-use space for individual work bookable in 30 minute slots and up to 3 hours maximum

View Photo →

🛋 Room 4.7 "Clerkenwell" - FOR SHARED USE

Social Lounge Space

A relaxed lounge-style room with sofas, board games and some sensory items available. Drop in and use at any point during the day.

View Photo →

✨ The Loft - FOR SHARED USE

Large Event & Social Space

A stunning space with panoramic views of Central London. Features seating for 18 (12 at a long table, 6 at a small table), additional sofa areas, and capacity for up to 60 people standing. Perfect for group socialising, quiet work, or study in a comfortable setting.

View Photo →

☕ Cafeteria & Refreshments

Free tea, coffee and water available. You're welcome to bring your own lunch and snacks.

⚠️ Limited to 20 attendees.

If you register, please ensure you follow our charity community guidelines that apply to all in-person initiatives.

We ask that you only register if you are confident you can attend. If you are unable to attend, please cancel your booking as soon as possible so that others can use the space.
