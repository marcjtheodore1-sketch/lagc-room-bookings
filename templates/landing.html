LAGC - Fridays @ Farringdon

London Autism Group Charity Weekly Initiative

This form is to register yourself for the London Autism Group Charity's 'LAGC Fridays @ Farringdon' weekly 11am-4pm initiative.

We're pleased to share that we've secured space at Pan MacMillan's Head Office as part of a new ongoing Fridays initiative.

📍 Venue

Pan Macmillan HQ

View on Google Maps →

The building is approximately a 5-minute walk from Farringdon Station (Cowcross Street exit).

♿

The building is wheelchair accessible. The environment is calm, quiet and comfortable.

👥

There will be at least two LAGC volunteers present throughout the day to support and coordinate the space.

📝

This is a corporate building and security requires us to provide a list of attendees in advance. When you arrive, you will need to sign in at reception.

⚠️ Important: If you are not registered in advance, you may not be permitted entry.

This initiative is for autistic adults aged 18 and over only.

📅 This Friday's Available Rooms

This is an ongoing, weekly initiative where we will have access to different types of spaces from week to week. Some Fridays will include social areas, smaller workspaces and larger shared environments. For this Friday, we have access to the following rooms:

🌊 Room 4.2 "Indigo"

Quiet Working & Body-Doubling Space

A shared calm room for focused work, planning, admin, studying or simply working alongside others quietly.

View Photo →

🌿 Room 4.4 "Rose"

Private Focus Room

A quiet single-use space for individual work. Managed in time blocks across the day, maximum 3 hours per person. Volunteers will coordinate usage on the day.

View Photo →

🛋 Room 4.7 "Clerkenwell"

Social Lounge Space

A relaxed lounge-style room with sofas, board games and some sensory items available. Drop in and use at any point during the day.

View Photo →

☕ Cafeteria & Refreshments

Free tea, coffee and water available. You're welcome to bring your own lunch and snacks.

⚠️ Limited to 20 attendees.

If you register, please ensure you follow our charity community guidelines that apply to all in-person initiatives.
